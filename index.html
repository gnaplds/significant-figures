Significant Figures Calculator

Number of significant figures: 0

Significant digits:

  • Non-zero digits: All non-zero digits are significant.
  • Leading zeros: Zeros before the first non-zero digit are not significant.
    Example: 0.0025 has two significant figures (2 and 5).
  • Captive zeros: Zeros between non-zero digits are significant.
    Example: 105 has three significant figures (1, 0, and 5).
  • Trailing zeros: Zeros to the right of a decimal point and after a non-zero digit are significant.
    Example: 3.00 has three significant figures (3, 0, and 0).
  • Trailing zeros in whole numbers: Are significant only if there is a decimal point.
    Example: 1500 has two significant figures, but 1500. has four significant figures.
